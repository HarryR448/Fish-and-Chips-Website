Navigation

To navigate the page simply go up to the navigation box which is below the box with the title in it and click on the words of the page you wish to go to and once you have done that you will be re-directed to the page you clicked the text for.

So if you click on Home you get brought to the homepage ,if you click Restaurant Menu you get brought to the menu page ,clicking gallery brings you to the gallery , clicking contact us to the contact us page and clicking the about us text brings you to the about us page.

Images

To view our images on our gallery or home page simply click on them and you will be re-directed to the file in its original full quality state.
