Welcome to our Website

We are weston fish and chips a small fish and chips store located in weston.

We serve a variety of foods including Cod, Chicken Nuggets, fish cake and chips among a variety of other foods , for our selection please check on the menu!

We offer both eating in store and takeaway.

Our staff are always working hard and you are welcome to pop in anytime.
