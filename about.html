About our Business

We are a small recently opened family business operating in weston where our fish and chips buisness was estalished in January 2015 on the side of our corner store.

Our restaurant has won a 5 in food hygiene as shown below as rated by the Food Standards Agency.

Our Aims

Our aim as a business is to provide tasty traditional fish and chips for as cheap and healthy as we can get them, while using only locally sourced ingredients in our food.

We aspire to grow in the future into a business with many stores throughout the midlands of England, and we hope to do this through hardwork, good products and the support of our customers.

In the past we have participated in a fundraiser for macmillan cancer research and helped with local cleanup events and we aim to support many more charitable and local activities in the future.
